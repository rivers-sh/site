how to pick the right distro for your needs

a quick google search on "before getting into linux, it's important to understand what linux is

firstly, linux is NOT an operating system, linux is the thing underneath the operating system (a kernel). but, when someone says "i run linux", they're talking about a linux distro

some quick info on what a distro is:

  • the operating system of your computer
  • there are hundreds of distros, each tailored to different needs
  • many distros are based off other distros, think of them as "forks"

if you want to jump right in, the best beginner friendly distro is linux mint. alternatively, if you're looking for something more specific to your needs, there's a subreddit called r/findmealinuxdistro
